Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I save on my energy costs?
Most businesses see savings of 15-30% on their energy costs through our competitive procurement process. Actual savings depend on your current rates, usage patterns, and market conditions at the time of procurement.
How long does the procurement process take?
We can often provide pricing on the spot (same day) for straightforward situations, or within 1-4 weeks depending on the complexity of your needs and market conditions. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your operations while ensuring you get the best rates available.
Do you charge upfront fees?
No, we typically don't charge upfront fees. Our compensation is usually structured through the savings we generate for your business, ensuring our interests are aligned with yours.
Can you help with multiple locations?
Yes, we specialize in multi-site energy management. We can coordinate procurement across all your locations, whether they're in the same market or spread across different regions.
What happens when my contract expires?
We proactively track all contract expiration dates and reach out 90-120 days before expiration to discuss renewal options. We'll run a new competitive event to ensure you continue getting the best rates.
Do you work with renewable energy suppliers?
Absolutely. We work with suppliers offering renewable energy options including solar, wind, and other green energy sources. We can help you evaluate renewable options and their impact on your costs and sustainability goals.
