Market Context: The "Penalty Box" & Tax Status

The CenterPoint 80% Ratchet: Because you hit a peak of 107.52 kVA, CenterPoint's "Penalty Box" rule dictates that your billing demand cannot drop below 80% of that peak (86.02 kVA) for the next 11 months. Even though your actual usage averages under 60 kVA, you are being billed for an extra 26 kVA every month—costing you roughly $239.08 in "ghost" demand charges.


Tax Exempt Status: As a manufacturing facility, you are likely eligible for a 100% exemption on state and local sales taxes for your electricity. Your current bill includes $246.93 in taxes. By signing and submitting the Texas Sales and Use Tax Exemption Certificate, we can waive these charges immediately.
