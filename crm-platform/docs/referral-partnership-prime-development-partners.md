Prepared for Nate / Prime Development Partners  
Prepared by Lewis / Nodal Point

## Purpose

This note lays out a simple referral partnership for new development opportunities tied to membership-based, multi-location brands like Glo Tanning and Earth Nail Spa.

The idea is straightforward:

- Prime Development Partners brings the opportunity.
- Nodal Point handles the energy side and closes the contract.
- Any new contract sourced through that referral is split 50/50.

## How The Referral Works

1. Prime Development Partners identifies a new opportunity.
2. Prime introduces Nodal Point to the prospect.
3. Nodal Point evaluates the energy usage and structures the contract.
4. If the contract closes, the referral payout is split 50/50.
5. The split applies only to new contracts that come through Prime’s referral.

## Payout Math

The working target is usually **10 mills per kWh**.

For clarity:

- 1 mill = $0.001
- So 10 mills = $0.01 per kWh

### Annual payout formula

**Annual payout = Annual kWh x Mills per kWh / 1,000**

### Example

If a site uses **1,000,000 kWh per year** and the deal is priced at **10 mills**:

- Annual payout = 1,000,000 x 10 / 1,000
- Annual payout = **$10,000**

If that payout is split 50/50:

- Prime Development Partners: **$5,000**
- Nodal Point: **$5,000**

### Quick examples

| Annual kWh | Mills | Annual payout | 50/50 split |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: |
| 1,000,000 | 10 | $10,000 | $5,000 / $5,000 |
| 2,000,000 | 10 | $20,000 | $10,000 / $10,000 |
| 3,500,000 | 10 | $35,000 | $17,500 / $17,500 |

## Simple Terms

- The target is 10 mills.
- The payout is based on the site’s annual kWh usage.
- If the final deal lands above or below 10 mills, the payout should follow the actual closed terms.
- Existing contracts that were already in motion before the introduction are not part of this split.
- The referral split applies after the contract is active and the payout is earned.

## Closing Position

This is a simple way for Prime Development Partners to earn on opportunities they already know about through their buildout and development work.

The goal is to make the referral clean:

- no confusion,
- no complicated formula,
- and a straight 50/50 split on new business they bring in.
