What we do

We reverse-engineer supplier tariffs to expose hidden cost leakage in commercial energy contracts.

Nodal Point is a forensic energy intelligence platform. We do not sell a generic commodity story. We give clients a sharp, evidence-backed view of where money is leaking, what the contract is really doing, and what the next move should be.
