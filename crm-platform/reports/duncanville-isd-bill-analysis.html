Leadership Action Plan

What needs to happen in the next 14 days

This page is written for sharing with supervisors and the superintendent. It focuses on action and cost protection, not technical terms.

Ask for written backup now
  • Ask Reliant/Oncor to show exactly why 175 kW was billed.
  • Ask which rule was used (minimum, look-back, or another utility billing rule).
  • Ask whether the same billing basis will be used next month.
If they confirm 175 kW is correct
Operational response

Treat this as a peak-demand control issue. Small operational changes can reduce future demand-related charges.

If they cannot justify 175 kW
Commercial response

Request billing correction or credit review immediately. Waiting can lock in repeated overcharges.

Ready-to-send message for Marquies
Email language

"We reviewed this invoice and the totals appear to add up. The open issue is why the bill charges demand at 175 kW while the measured peak shown is 154 kW. Please provide the billing determinant details and the exact tariff rule used for this charge."
