Why This Fits

  • Prime Development Partners already does commercial construction and franchise buildouts.
  • Glo Tanning is clearly a growth brand, with 358 public locations and a franchising section.
  • Earth Nail Spa runs a membership model and shows waitlist and franchise language on its public site.

How The Referral Works

  1. Prime brings the opportunity.
  2. Nodal Point handles the energy side and structures the contract.
  3. If the contract closes, the referral payout is split 50/50.
  4. This applies only to new contracts introduced by Prime.

Simple Terms

  • Target pricing is usually 10 mills.
  • If the final deal lands above or below 10 mills, the payout follows the actual closed terms.
  • Existing deals already in motion stay out of the split.

Brand Context

  • Prime Development Partners Commercial real estate construction, tenant improvements, MEP plans, and franchise buildouts.
  • Glo Tanning National scale, 358 locations, franchising, and membership pricing that starts at $19.95 per month.
  • Earth Nail Spa Membership-driven concept with public pricing at $129 per month and $199 per month, plus a waitlist.

Payout Math

Rule
1 mill = $0.001, so 10 mills = $0.01 per kWh.
Formula
Annual payout = annual kWh x mills / 1,000
Example
1,000,000 kWh x 10 mills = $10,000 annual payout.
Split
$5,000 / $5,000 on a 50/50 referral split.
